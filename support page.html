Knowledge Base

How do I reset my password?

To reset your password, click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. Enter your email address, and we'll send you a password reset link. Follow the instructions in the email to create a new password.

Can I get a refund for a course I purchased?

Yes, we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for all course purchases. If you're not satisfied with a course, you can request a full refund within 30 days of purchase by contacting our support team.

How do I track my learning progress?

You can track your learning progress through your personalized dashboard. The dashboard displays your enrolled courses, completion status, recent activities, and achievement badges. You can also generate detailed progress reports.

Can I download course materials for offline use?

Yes, most course materials are available for download. Look for the download icon next to the course resources. Downloaded materials can be accessed offline through our mobile app or saved to your device for reference.

How do I get a certificate after completing a course?

Certificates are automatically issued upon successful completion of a course. You must complete all required modules and pass the final assessment with a score of at least 70%. Certificates can be accessed from your achievements section and can be downloaded as PDF or shared directly to your LinkedIn profile.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover), PayPal, and bank transfers. For some regions, we also support local payment methods like Apple Pay, Google Pay, and various regional payment processors.

How can I become an instructor on Knowledge Core?

To become an instructor, visit our "Teach on Knowledge Core" page and submit an application. We look for experts with deep knowledge in their field and the ability to create engaging content. After approval, you'll gain access to our course creation tools and instructor resources.
