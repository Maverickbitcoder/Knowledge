Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

To reset your password, click the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. Enter your email address, and we'll send you a password reset link. Click the link in the email and follow the instructions to create a new password. For security reasons, password reset links expire after 24 hours.

Can I download courses for offline viewing?

Yes, you can download course materials for offline viewing through our mobile app. Open the course you want to download, tap the download icon next to each lecture or section you wish to save offline. Downloaded content will be available in the "Downloads" section of the app. Note that downloads expire after 30 days if you don't connect to the internet.

How do I get a certificate after completing a course?

Certificates are automatically issued when you complete all required components of a course (including quizzes, assignments, and projects). Once you've met all requirements, your certificate will appear in the "Certificates" section of your profile. You can download it as a PDF or share it directly to LinkedIn from there. Some advanced courses may require passing a final exam with a minimum score.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover), PayPal, and Apple Pay. In select regions, we also support local payment methods such as bank transfers. For corporate or team subscriptions, we offer invoice-based payments. All transactions are securely processed and encrypted.

Can I switch between subscription plans?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your subscription at any time from your account settings. When upgrading, the new plan takes effect immediately, and you'll be charged a prorated amount for the remainder of your billing cycle. When downgrading, the changes will take effect at the end of your current billing period. There are no penalties for changing plans.
